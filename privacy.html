Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2, 2026

Summary

PricePilot helps you find an optimal price using the data you enter. We use Firebase for authentication (including Google sign-in) and we store your product and sales inputs in a database (Firebase Firestore) so you can access them across devices. This policy explains what we collect, how we use it, and your choices.

Who We Are

“PricePilot”, “we”, “us” refers to the PricePilot project and its operator.

Information We Collect

  • Account data: If you sign in with Google, we receive basic profile information from Google via Firebase (typically your name, email address, and profile photo). If you use email/password (if enabled), we collect your email and authentication credentials via Firebase.
  • Product and pricing inputs: Product names, cost, price points, units sold, timestamps, and any other fields you enter to generate recommendations (“User Content”).
  • Device and usage data: Basic technical data such as IP address, browser type, and error logs generated by the app and/or our infrastructure for security and debugging. (If we enable analytics in the future, we will update this policy.)
  • Local storage: Some settings or cached data may be stored in your browser (for example, localStorage) to improve your experience.

How We Use Information

  • Provide and operate the service (sign-in, saving products, syncing data, generating recommendations).
  • Maintain security, prevent abuse, and troubleshoot bugs.
  • Improve the product (e.g., understanding feature usage and performance).
  • Communicate with you about support requests or important updates.

Legal Bases (Where Applicable)

Depending on where you live, we process your information based on: providing the service you request (contract), our legitimate interests (security and improvement), compliance with legal obligations, and/or your consent (where required, e.g., certain analytics cookies).

How We Share Information

We do not sell your personal information. We share information only as needed:

  • Service providers: Firebase/Google (authentication, hosting, database, and related cloud services) and other providers we may use for infrastructure, logging, or email delivery.
  • Legal and safety: If required by law or to protect rights, safety, and security.
  • Business changes: If the project is reorganized or transferred, information may be transferred as part of that transaction.

Data Storage and Security

We store account data and saved inputs in Firebase/Google Cloud services (including Firebase Authentication and Firestore). We use reasonable technical measures to protect data, but no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

Data Retention

We keep account data and stored inputs for as long as you maintain an account or as needed to provide the service. We may retain limited records for legal, security, or operational purposes.

Your Choices and Rights

  • Access/update: You can review and update your stored product data within the app (where available).
  • Delete: You can delete stored records in the app (where available). Account deletion and deletion of stored data may be available in-app depending on the feature set.
  • Cookies/local storage: You can control storage via your browser settings and (where applicable) cookie banners/settings.

If you are in a region with privacy laws (e.g., the EEA/UK/California), you may have additional rights such as data portability or objection.

International Transfers

Your information may be processed in countries other than where you live, depending on our service providers’ infrastructure. We take steps designed to ensure appropriate safeguards where required.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will update the “Last updated” date and, if changes are material, we will provide additional notice.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at pricepilotcontact@gmail.com.
